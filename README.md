# Sustainable Operations & Product Strategy Portfolio

Welcome to my portfolio of sustainable global operations, green logistics, and impact-driven product management frameworks developed during the M1 Gaïa program of my Master's in Management. This repository demonstrates how corporate strategy, ethical leadership, and product innovation intersect to solve complex modern business challenges.

---

## Project 1: Decarbonizing Freight Transport & Maritime Logistics
**File:** `G01_Shushma_Transportation_RT.pdf`

A strategic round-table analysis evaluating the systemic restructuring of global maritime shipping logistics to drastically minimize carbon footprints without disrupting international trade security.

### Core Operational Insights
* **Alternative Fuel Integration:** Evaluated the commercial viability, safety regulations, and infrastructure requirements for next-generation clean energies in maritime freight, including green methanol, ammonia, and Small Modular Nuclear Reactors (SMRs).
* **Systemic Supply Chain Re-engineering:** Shifted focus from simple incremental operational improvements toward deep systemic updates, including carbon accounting mechanisms, wind-propelled cargo ships (e.g., *Grain de Sail* models), and local sourcing loops.
* **Public-Private Stakeholder Collaboration:** Frameworked policy avenues for cross-border government and private sector alliances to accelerate clean supply chain adoption under rising ESG regulatory pressures.

### Key Competencies Demonstrated
* Sustainable Supply Chain Management (Green Logistics)
* Environmental Risk Assessment & Alternative Fuel Strategy
* Cross-Border Regulatory Navigation & Ethical Operations Leadership

## Project 2: Impact-Driven Product Management Frameworks
**File:** `G01-Shushma-Report-Impact Job.pdf`

A career-mapping field report investigating the modern Product Manager (PM) lifecycle, detailing how customer-centric innovation, cross-functional alignment, and agile problem-solving drive sustainable business growth.

### Core Product Strategy Insights
* **Cross-Functional Orchestration:** Analyzed methodologies used by top-tier PMs to bridge the communication gaps between Engineering, Marketing, Sales, and Corporate Operations to establish a unified product vision.
* **Data-Driven & Agile Problem Solving:** Documented real-world strategic frameworks, including cross-functional workshop execution, risk mitigation models, and turning customer friction points into long-term product roadmaps.
* **Value-Oriented Business Metrics:** Evaluated how strategic product positioning balances short-term corporate profitability with long-term brand equity, user satisfaction, and customer retention.

### Key Competencies Demonstrated
* Product Lifecycle Management & Strategy Mapping
* Cross-Functional Team Leadership & Stakeholder Management
* Agile Problem Solving, Qualitative Field Interviewing, & Risk Mitigation
